Lending Club specialises in lending various types of loans to urban customers.Borrowers can easily access lower interest rate loans through a fast online interface. When the company receives a loan application, the company has to make a decision for loan approval based on the applicants profile. When a person applies for a loan,the company need to estimate their eligibilty criterias based on 2 possible scenarios described below:

Fully paid: Applicant has fully paid the loan (the principal and the interest rate)

Charged-off: Applicant has not paid the instalments in due time for a long period of time, i.e. he/she has defaulted on the loan
